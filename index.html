Welcome to the ECMWF Pensioners Association

Connecting pensioners from the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts

About Our Association

The ECMWF Pensioners Association was established to maintain and strengthen the connections between former staff members of the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts. Our association serves as a vibrant community for retired colleagues who have contributed to the advancement of weather forecasting and climate research throughout their careers.

Our Mission

We aim to:

  • Maintain connections with the Centre and stay informed about its developments
  • Organize social events, and activities for our members
  • Provide a platform for sharing experiences and staying in touch
  • Support members in their retirement journey

Membership

Membership is open to all former ECMWF staff members who have retired from the Centre and are receiving a pension. This also includes people receiving reversion pensions.

Activities and Benefits

Our association organizes regular activities including:

  • Social gatherings
  • Newsletters with updates from the Centre and member news
  • Access to private members' documents and resources
