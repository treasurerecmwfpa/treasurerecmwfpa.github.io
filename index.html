About Our Association

The ECMWF Pensioners Association was established to maintain and strengthen the connections between former staff members of the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts.

Our Mission

We aim to:

  • Foster continued fellowship among ECMWF retirees
  • Maintain connections with the Centre and stay informed about its developments
  • Organize social events, reunions, and activities for our members
